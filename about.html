About Regen Studio

Innovations that regenerate humans, cities, and nature

Regen Studio is a Netherlands-based innovation design studio specialising in Digital Product Passports, EU circular economy regulations, and regenerative innovation for governments, industry, and research institutes.

We are a regenerative innovation design studio pioneering systemic change at the intersection of technology, society, and nature. From The Netherlands and Brazil, we help organisations design innovations that create lasting positive impact.

What we believe

The most impactful innovations don't just solve problems — they regenerate the systems they touch. Through inspiration, beauty, and years of expertise, we transform systems so they work for an age of humans living in balance with themselves, their communities, their cities, and their planet.

We design innovations from a systemic perspective. The most successful innovations which manage to change systems and generate positive impact combine technological, social, political, and natural elements.

Our focus

Energy Transition

Decentralised, locally managed grids that go beyond renewables toward a just energy future.

  • Smart Grids
  • Energy Communities
  • Energy Justice

Circular Economy

Closing resource loops so products and materials stay in use longer, eliminating waste by design.

  • Digital Product Passports
  • Circular Business Models

Liveable Cities

Habitats where people and nature thrive together through responsible, human-centred technology.

  • Living Labs
  • Digital Participation
  • Urban Greening

Resilient Nature

Reversing ecological destruction with technology that restores ecosystems and protects biodiversity.

  • Reforestation
  • Biodiversity
  • Regenerative Agriculture

Digital Society

Privacy-first systems where data serves people, protecting individuals in an increasingly digital world.

  • Digital Identity
  • Privacy-by-Design
  • AI
